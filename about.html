About Igor

Meet Igor Korchilov

Igor Korchilov was born in Murmansk, Russia. He attended the First Moscow State Institute of Foreign Languages, trained in simultaneous interpretation at the UN-sponsored course for translators and interpreters in Moscow, and completed advanced studies at the Foreign Ministry Diplomatic Academy. He speaks Russian, English, French, and Spanish, and has been an interpreter at the highest national and international levels for 29 years. He has worked as personal interpreter for Mikhail Gorbachev, Margaret Thatcher, Ronald Reagan, George H.W. Bush, and Leonid Brezhnev, among countless other world leaders and heads of state.
